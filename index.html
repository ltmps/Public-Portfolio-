Background

Mechanical and mechatronics engineer with about three years of hands-on experience designing, building, and testing hardware systems in robotics, rocketry, HVAC building systems, and consumer products.

My work spans cryogenic liquid rocket feed systems and high-pressure test rigs, free-flying robotic platforms, commercial VRF/heat pump designs, and compact evaporative coolers. I take systems from first principles through CAD, fabrication, assembly, structured testing, and design iteration — with a strong focus on safety, reliability, and clear documentation.
